# KASIRKU UMKM - PORTONEXT DOCUMENTATION

## 1. System Overview
PortoNext is a specialized Point of Sale (POS) and Inventory Management System designed for UMKM. It focuses on physical stock organization, allowing business owners to track not just "how much" they have, but "where" it is located (Warehouse vs. Specific Racks).

## 2. Key Modules & Features

### A. Dashboard Overview
- Real-time statistics of total products, active members, and today's income.
- Visual status of storage rooms (occupancy percentage).

### B. Product & Inventory Management
- **Universal Inventory**: Single list of all products.
- **Refill Feature**: Quick stock addition directly to the warehouse.
- **Micro-Management**: Change warehouse stock levels manually for corrections.
- **Inventory Detail Page**: Specific dashboard for each product showing:
  - Warehouse availability.
  - Distribution across multiple "Custom Rooms" (Display Racks).
  - Quick transfer between Warehouse and Racks.

### C. Logistics & Custom Rooms
- **Custom Room Creation**: Define physical storage areas (e.g., "Rak Bakso", "Dimsum Hot").
- **Capacity Tracking**: Monitor how many units each room can hold.
- **Room-Level Detail**: View exactly what products are in a specific room and move them back to the warehouse if needed.

### D. Point of Sale (POS)
- Fast checkout system for transactions.
- Automatic stock deduction from the primary location.

## 3. Roles & Permissions
- **Administrator**: Full system access (User management, Category creation, Global settings, Financial reports).
- **Staff/Cashier**: Access to POS, inventory viewing, and stock movement.

## 4. Page Architecture
- `/dashboard/products`: Main inventory grid.
- `/dashboard/products/add`: New product registration.
- `/dashboard/products/inventory/[id]`: [Specialized] Stock refill and physical location distribution.
- `/dashboard/locations`: Management of "Custom Rooms".
- `/dashboard/locations/[id]`: Contents of a specific room.
- `/dashboard/pos`: Transaction interface.
- `/dashboard/transactions`: History of sales.

# GITHUB DESCRIPTION (Bahasa Indonesia)

**PortoNext - Sistem Manajemen Inventaris & POS UMKM Modern**

PortoNext adalah solusi all-in-one untuk pelaku UMKM yang membutuhkan ketelitian dalam pengelolaan stok fisik. Berbeda dengan POS standar, PortoNext mengusung konsep "Multi-Location Inventory" yang memungkinkan pengusaha membagi stok antara Gudang (Warehouse) dan Rak Jualan (Display Rooms) secara dinamis.

**Fitur Unggulan:**
- 📦 **Manajemen Stok Dual-Layer**: Pisahkan stok gudang dan stok siap jual.
- 🏗️ **Custom Room Management**: Buat rak atau area display kustom dengan indikator kapasitas real-time.
- 🔄 **Quick Refill & Transfer**: Tambah stok hanya dalam satu klik dan pindahkan antar lokasi dengan mudah.
- 📊 **Visual Dashboard**: Pantau kesehatan bisnis dan okupansi rak melalui antarmuka premium.
- 🛒 **POS Terintegrasi**: Transaksi cepat yang memotong stok secara akurat.
- 🚀 **Build with Modern Tech**: Astro, React, Tailwind CSS, Prisma, and PostgreSQL.

Dibuat dengan fokus pada pengalaman pengguna (UX) yang intuitif dan desain visual yang profesional untuk meningkatkan kelas bisnis UMKM Anda.
